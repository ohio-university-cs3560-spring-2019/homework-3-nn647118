\section{Standard Deviation Formal Definition}

Standard deviation is a measure of the amount of variation or dispersion of a set of data values. The equation is as follows:

\begin{equation}
	\sigma = \sqrt{\frac{\sum {(x_i-\mu) }^2}{N}}
\end{equation}
